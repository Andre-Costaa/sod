Strategic Concern: Conversion Gap

However, the near-zero commercial intent representation (0.5%) indicates a failure to capture users at the consideration/decision stages of the funnel. This limits business value generation from organic traffic.

Recommendations

  1. Add commercial intent modifiers: Target "best," "comparison," "review" variants of core topics
  2. Create service-oriented content: "How to implement," "consulting," "solutions" pages
  3. Optimize conversion paths: Add CTAs in informational content linking to commercial pages
  4. Monitor the 4 commercial keywords: Prioritize these in quick win optimizations
